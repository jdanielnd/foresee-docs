The forecasts you generate with Foresee are filed into projects, which you can access at any time from your desktop, tablet or smartphone.

Moreover, Foresee allows you to export the results of your forecasts into CSV files, which can be opened in Microsoft Excel Â®. If you prefer, results can also be converted into PDF reports that contain plots and tables of your forecasts.

To access the forecasts stored within a project, click on the name of the project you want to view.

You will be directed to the project page that you selected.

  1. Clicking on CSV, located to the right of "Historical Data", will allow you to access the file containing the data used to generate the forecasts in that particular project.
  2. Clicking CSV, located to the right of "Forecasts", will allow you to access the CSV file with all the forecasts in this project.
  3. Clicking on PDF, located to the right of "Forecasts", will allow you to access a report in PDF format with plots and tables covering all the forecasts in this project.
  4. Clicking on CSV next to a particular series, will allow you to access the CSV file with forecasts from that series.
  5. Clicking on PDF next to a particular series, will allow you to access a report in PDF format with the plot and table relating to that series.
  6. Hovering your mouse pointer over the name of a series, will bring up a trashcan icon on the right side of the window. Clicking on this icon will allow you to delete the selected series. This same procedure applies to projects that you have created. On the project page, hover the mouse over the name of a project and click on the trashcan to delete that particular project.
